    Welcome to Haegen Tech Remote Laboratory

    Haegen Tech Remote Laboratory is an initiative taken by students from Program Kejuruteraan Electric (PKE) to coduct experiments remotely. You can now do your experiments wihout having to commute to the lab. The program is under PKE or the ELectrical, Electronics and Systems Engineering Department of the National University of Malaysia (UKM).

    How to use Haegen Tech Remote Lab

    General Procedure:

  • Select the workstation of your choice.
  • Slect the experiment of your Choice, For example:
  • Rectifier.
  • Digital Logic Gates.
  • RLC
  • Download the Lab Moduel.
  • CLick on generate Lab.

    • What to Expect?

    Schedule your lab based on your custom time table. However, allocation of a time slot will solely subject to availability of workstations

    If the current workstation is full, you may check the other workstations. In the Event that all workstations are full, you may leave your details with the administrations and the your prefered lab title. Our Staff will slot you in as soon as possible
